◈ Contributor Program

BECOME A
CONTRIBUTOR

The Cheeseburger Database is built by approved contributors — people who eat cheeseburgers and have something to say about them. Reviews are posted to Bluesky in a structured format and indexed here automatically. The review lives on your profile. We record it. Nobody owns the data but you.

◈ How it works
01
Apply below
Fill out the application. We verify you're a real person with a Bluesky account. Approved handles go live within 48 hours.
02
Learn the format
Read the format guide. Six lines, one photo, exact structure. The parser is unforgiving — follow it precisely and everything is automatic.
03
Post to Bluesky, get indexed
Any post from your approved handle tagged #CBDB in the correct format is indexed within the hour. The entry belongs to you — it lives on your Bluesky permanently.
